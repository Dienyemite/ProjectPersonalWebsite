About

Sadman Mazumder

Developer & Artist | New Jersey Institute of Technology

Hello! Welcome to my website. I love Water Lilies, which back home we refer to as Shapla, it speaks to the artsy side of me, so I have my site with that aesthetic in mind.

I'm an undergrad at New Jersey Institute of Technology with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology and a minor in Computer Science. I'm deeply passionate about technology and the arts, and using them in conjunction as a means to solve problems and express stories.

My work spans into multiple areas, some of which include Web Development, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and Game Development. I leverage these skills to make a meaningful impact in the industry.

As a Lead STEM Instructor at the RChild with nearly three years of experience teaching hundreds of students, I've able to communicate complex concepts with ease. It is a skill that carries over into every project I undertake.

My goal is to become a Software Engineer, continuously learning and building the things that will impact the world. I additionally aspire to be a Game Developer, as I'm passionate about using gaming as a means of expressing meaningful and engrossing stories.

Creativity and the Art of it all is deeply important to me, illustrated from the design of my site to the Art portfolio included. I aim to bring the imagination and wonders of the arts with technology to create memorable and impactful experiences.
